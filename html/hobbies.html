Additional Interests

FENCING

I've been fencing since January of 2004, inspired to recreate the Cliffs of Insanity dueling scene from The Princess Bride for my high school theater class. I enjoyed it so much I started coaching and officiating the next year, and have been in the fencing community ever since.

CLIMBING

Started rock climbing as "training" for a Tough Mudder in 2013. Had so much fun I never stopped and started teaching technique to climbers in our Meetup group.
